Welcome

I am very pleased to welcome you in this site. I admire you to be the part of this effort of educational development through Education Today.

My motivation to establish this "Education Today" website are my students. My students who believed me as a teacher, there is my responsibility to honor it and fulfil it.

I always remember the quote of Margret Mead "Children must be taught how to think, not what to think". Within this statement, I get philosophy of education. I cannot forget Nelson Mandela who emphasize on power of education over other qualities “A good head and good heart are always a formidable combination. But when you add to that a literate tongue or pen, then you have something very special.” I realized that I have to contribute in education from my side. I learnt from my father, make every moment educational. Similar quote of Charles M. Schulz is supporting me, “Try not to have a good time...this is supposed to be educational.” I determined myself, I make my moments educational. This is the reason why I launched this website.

Major contents of this website are:
  1. Support to the teacher and pedagogy.
  2. Support to community school enhancemet.
  3. Quality education
  4. Teacher service commission related
  5. Educational Articles
  6. Educational information/news
  7. Publications and downloads
  8. On demand contents.
  9. Research findings - authentic sources
  10. My own views

To focus those teachers and students who are competing in Teacher Service Commission Examination, a TSC Preparation tab is created. There you can find resources useful for teacher-competitors.

I would like to invite my visitors to share their articles through this page. At last, I would like to inform you that I will try to incorporate authentic resources as far as possible.

Thank you for visiting this site.
